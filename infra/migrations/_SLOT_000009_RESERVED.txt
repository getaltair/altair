Slot 000009 is intentionally empty.

During Feature 003 (Server Core), entity_tags was initially assigned slot 000009 but
was renumbered to 000025 to resolve FK ordering constraints (entity_tags references
several tables that are created in slots 000010–000024).

sqlx migrates by timestamp/version, not by sequential position, so gaps are harmless.
The next available slot after 000025 is 000026.
