## TODO: What This Document Should Contain

### Purpose
Answer common questions from users, contributors, and curious people learning about Altair.

### Required Sections

1. **General Questions**
   - What is Altair?
   - Who is Altair for?
   - How is it different from Todoist/Notion/etc?
   - Is it really free?
   - What does "ADHD-friendly" mean?

2. **Getting Started**
   - How do I sign up?
   - Is there a mobile app?
   - Can I import from other tools?
   - Do I need to learn anything complex?
   - What if I don't have ADHD?

3. **Features**
   - What is quick capture?
   - How does AI task breakdown work?
   - What is focus mode?
   - Can I collaborate with others? (not yet, explain roadmap)
   - Does it work offline?

4. **Privacy & Security**
   - Is my data private?
   - Can I self-host?
   - Do you sell my data? (NO!)
   - What analytics do you collect?
   - Is it encrypted?

5. **Technical Questions**
   - What's the tech stack?
   - Can I contribute?
   - Is there an API?
   - What platforms are supported?
   - System requirements?

6. **Pricing & Business Model**
   - Is it really free?
   - How will you make money?
   - What about premium features?
   - Can I donate?

7. **ADHD-Specific Questions**
   - I've tried productivity apps before and failed. Why will this work?
   - What if I forget to use it?
   - Will this cure my ADHD? (No, it's a tool)
   - Can neurotypical people use it?
   - How do you handle time blindness?

8. **Troubleshooting**
   - App won't sync
   - Forgot my password
   - Lost all my tasks (panic!)
   - App is slow
   - Something's broken
