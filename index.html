Why Altair?

Traditional project management tools are built for neurotypical brains. They assume infinite executive function, consistent focus, and linear thinking.

Altair is different. It's designed from the ground up for the way ADHD brains actually work— embracing hyperfocus, supporting task switching, and reducing decision fatigue at every turn.

ADHD users don't need to be fixed. They need tools that work with their brains.

Built in Public

Altair is being built openly by the community. Follow the development process, contribute ideas, and help shape a tool that actually serves neurodivergent needs.
