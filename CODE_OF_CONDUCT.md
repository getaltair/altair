# Code of Conduct

## TL;DR

**Be kind, be respectful, and be ADHD-aware.**

This is a neurodiverse-friendly space. We support each other and assume good intentions.

---

## Our Pledge

We want Altair's community to be:

- **Welcoming** - Everyone is valued
- **Safe** - Free from harassment
- **Supportive** - Help each other succeed
- **Neurodiverse-friendly** - ADHD-aware communication

**As contributors and maintainers, we pledge to:**

- Respect all people regardless of experience level, neurodiversity, race,
  ethnicity, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, age, religion, or
  other identity
- Use positive and clear language
- Accept constructive feedback graciously
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy and patience

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful** - Disagree without being disagreeable
- **Be patient** - Not everyone processes information the same way
- **Be constructive** - Offer solutions, not just criticism
- **Be clear** - Use scannable, ADHD-friendly communication
- **Assume good intent** - People make mistakes

### ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment** - Intimidation, stalking, or unwelcome attention
- **Discrimination** - Based on any protected characteristic
- **Personal attacks** - Attack ideas, not people
- **Trolling** - Deliberately inflammatory or off-topic comments
- **Privacy violations** - Sharing others' private information
- **Spam** - Excessive self-promotion or irrelevant content

---

## ADHD-Specific Considerations

### What ADHD-Aware Means

Many community members have ADHD. This means:

- **We might forget things** - Gentle reminders are appreciated
- **We communicate differently** - Direct and clear is helpful
- **We might hyperfocus** - Sometimes we dive deep and lose track of time
- **We have executive function challenges** - Structure and clarity help

### How to Be Supportive

- **Use clear headers and bullets** - Scannable text helps everyone
- **Be direct** - Say what you mean clearly
- **Provide structure** - Step-by-step instructions work better
- **Allow time** - Not everyone can respond immediately
- **Assume positive intent** - ADHD traits aren't character flaws

**We don't excuse bad behavior,** but we do recognize that communication
differences exist.

## Enforcement

### Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **For urgent/serious issues:**

   - Contact project maintainers at [robert@getaltair.app]
   - Include details: what happened, when, who was involved

2. **For minor issues:**
   - Address directly if comfortable
   - Or report to maintainers

### What Happens Next

1. **Report received** - We'll acknowledge within 48 hours
2. **Investigation** - We review the situation
3. **Action** - Appropriate response based on severity
4. **Follow-up** - We inform the reporter of the outcome

### Possible Actions

Depending on severity:

- Private warning
- Public warning
- Temporary ban from community spaces
- Permanent ban

**We aim for restorative justice** when possible, but serious violations result
in immediate removal.
