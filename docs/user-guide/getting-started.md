### Writing Guidelines

**ADHD-Friendly Format:**

- ✅ Numbered steps for procedures
- ✅ Screenshots for every major step
- ✅ Highlight keyboard shortcuts in boxes
- ✅ Use emojis for visual anchors
- ✅ TL;DR at the top
- ✅ "Can't focus? Skip to..." boxes
- ✅ Progress indicators (Step 1 of 5)

**Tone:**
- Encouraging without being patronizing
- Celebrate small wins
- Normalize ADHD struggles
- No jargon
- Assume intelligence, not prior knowledge

## Notes for Future Writer

**Key Points:**
- This is often the FIRST thing new users read - make it count!
- Every paragraph should answer "how does this help my ADHD?"
- Screenshots should show success, not complexity
- End on an encouraging note

**Common Pitfalls to Avoid:**
- ❌ Information overload in the first guide
- ❌ Assuming users know project management terms
- ❌ Not enough visual aids
- ❌ Steps that are too complex
- ❌ Forgetting mobile users

**User Testing Questions:**
- Could you complete signup without help?
- Did you capture your first task within 5 minutes?
- What was confusing?
- What made you excited?
- Would you recommend Altair to a friend?
